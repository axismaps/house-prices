House Prices: 2010 to Present

This map shows changing house prices from 2010 to present by postcode. It looks at changing sale prices for individual properties, instead of changing median price for all properties in the postcode.

We looked at sales between 2010 - 2013 and 2014 - Present and calculated the difference in sale price then took the median difference by postcode to get a final value. The resulting map shows what we would expect, tremendous gains in London with the North lagging behind or losing value.
